# Gurumbé Capital Design System v2

## Visual Strategy: The Vibrant Corridor
The updated system moves from a purely "bookish" editorial feel to a high-contrast, digitally authoritative presence. It merges the institutional gravity of the initial design with the vibrant colors of the corridor's geography—incorporating deep greens and clay reds alongside the signature solar gold.

## Color Evolution
- **Base:** The primary near-black (#0f0c09) remains the ground, representing the soil and the depth of history.
- **Solar Gold (#c8841d):** Retained as the primary energy source, signaling precision and growth.
- **Deep Forest Green (#2d5a27):** Introduced for the "Energy" and "Nature" aspects of the corridor. It represents the fertility of the focus markets and the green energy transition.
- **Terracotta Red (#b5531d):** Representing the clay, the people, and the physical real estate. It's the color of the earth and human craft.

## Typography Evolution
We've moved to heavier weights (500-600) for headers to provide more "web" presence and authority. The type scale is increased to feel more cinematic and less like a printed page. DM Mono remains the architectural anchor for data and navigation.

## Communication as Investment
The layout should prioritize "The Long Read" and "Cinematic Visuals." Every section is framed as a "Sovereign Story." Large, bold headlines act as declarations.
